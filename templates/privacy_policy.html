Privacy Policy

Welcome to Wake Up. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

To provide you with our risk management and portfolio tracking services, we collect and process the following information:

  • Email address: Used for account creation, login, and important communications about your account.
  • Name: Helps us personalize your experience and address you appropriately.
  • Password: Securely encrypted using industry-standard methods to protect your account.
  • User preferences and settings: Including your portfolio configurations and display preferences.
  • Portfolio and investment data: Information about your investments, benchmarks, and risk factors that you choose to track.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is essential for providing and improving our services. We use it to:

  • Provide and maintain our services: Your data enables us to deliver personalized portfolio management and risk analysis tools.
  • Process your transactions: We use your information to track and manage your investment portfolios.
  • Send important updates: We may notify you about significant changes to our services or security updates.
  • Improve our services: We analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience and develop new features.

3. Cookies and Remember Me

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience and provide essential functionality:

  • Authentication cookies: When you choose "Remember Me" during login, we store a secure cookie that keeps you signed in for 30 days.
  • Session cookies: These temporary cookies help maintain your session while you're actively using our service.
  • Preference cookies: Store your display preferences and settings for a more personalized experience.

You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings at any time. However, please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our service.

4. Data Security

We take your data security seriously and implement multiple layers of protection:

  • Encryption: All sensitive data, including passwords, is encrypted using industry-standard methods.
  • Secure connections: We use HTTPS to ensure all data transmission is encrypted.
  • Regular security audits: Our systems undergo frequent security reviews and updates.
  • Access controls: Strict internal policies limit access to your personal information.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have control over your personal information:

  • Access your data: You can view and download your personal information through your account settings.
  • Update information: You can modify your profile information at any time.
  • Delete your account: You can request account deletion, which will remove all your personal data.
  • Cookie preferences: You can manage your cookie settings through your browser.
